**Nationwide Vulnerability Concerns Agency (NAVCA â€“ South Sudan)** is a South Sudanese, Christian and women-led organization with its Head Office in **Jebel (Jeel) Market, Juba, Central Equatoria State**.

NAVCA was established in recognition of the prolonged humanitarian, socio-economic, and governance challenges faced by communities in South Sudan following years of conflict and instability. The organization prioritizes vulnerable groups who have historically been marginalized, particularly **women, children, youth, the elderly, and persons with disabilities**.

NAVCA operates on principles of **human dignity, equity, justice, accountability, and partnership**, delivering humanitarian and development interventions regardless of race, tribe, religion, gender, or political affiliation.
