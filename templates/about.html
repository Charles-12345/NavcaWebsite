About NAVCA

NAVCA is a Christian and women-led organization based in South Sudan with head office in Jebel/Jeel Market, Juba.

Preamble

NAVCA was established in response to the vulnerability experienced by communities in South Sudan following years of conflict and limited access to basic services. NAVCA prioritizes vulnerable populations—especially women and children—recognizing systemic barriers that have historically limited women’s access to ownership, opportunity, and justice.

Vision

To foster a safe, peaceful, and inclusive society that upholds human dignity, protects the environment and wildlife, promotes access to basic socio-economic services, and advances good governance, the rule of law, gender equality, and human rights.

Mission

NAVCA shall endeavour to improve the quality of living of the most vulnerable communities and groups in South Sudan with emphasis to women, children, the elderly, and people living with disability.

Core Values

  • Christian stewardship
  • Vulnerability, equity, and justice for all
  • Transparency, integrity, and accountability
  • Dignity and rights of persons with special needs
  • Partnership and responsiveness

Non-Discrimination

The organization renders services wherever need arises, regardless of race/tribe, religion, gender, or political affiliation, in South Sudan and beyond.
