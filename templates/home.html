Nationwide Vulnerability Concerns Agency (NAVCA)

A Christian and women-led National NGO based in South Sudan, working to improve the quality of life for vulnerable communities—especially women, children, youth, the elderly, and persons with disabilities.

Explore Programs Partner With Us

Vision

To foster a safe, peaceful, and inclusive society that upholds human dignity, promotes access to basic services, and advances good governance, rule of law, gender equality, and human rights.

Mission

To improve the quality of living of the most vulnerable communities and groups in South Sudan, with emphasis on women, children, the elderly, and people living with disability.

Thematic Areas

Protection, GBV & Child Protection

Safety, prevention, response, and psychosocial support.

Food Security & Livelihoods

Skills, income generation, self-reliance and resilience.

Education

Formal and non-formal education and vocational training.

Data Collection & Research

Evidence generation, assessments, monitoring and learning.

Peacebuilding

Dialogue, reconciliation and social cohesion activities.

Humanitarian Assistance

Life-saving support in crisis and hard-to-reach areas.

Areas of Operation

Greater Unity, Jonglei, Greater Upper Nile, Greater Equatoria, Greater Bahr el Ghazal—depending on access and funding.
