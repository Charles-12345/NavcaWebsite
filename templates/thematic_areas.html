Thematic Areas

NAVCA works to enhance resilience and well-being of vulnerable populations across South Sudan through inclusive access to education, livelihoods, protection services, peacebuilding, and humanitarian assistance.

Protection, GBV & Child Protection

  • GBV prevention and response
  • Child protection and case management
  • Psychosocial support and safe referrals

Food Security & Livelihoods

  • Skills development and income generation
  • Economic empowerment (including VSLA)
  • Self-reliance and resilience strengthening

Education

  • Formal and non-formal education
  • Technical and vocational skills training
  • Inclusion of youth, adults, and persons with disabilities

Data Collection & Research

  • Assessments, monitoring, evaluation, and learning
  • Evidence generation and research support
  • Third Party Monitoring for transparency and credibility

Peacebuilding & Humanitarian Assistance

  • Community dialogue, reconciliation, and social cohesion
  • Life-saving relief support in crisis-affected areas
  • Support to IDPs, returnees, and marginalized rural communities
